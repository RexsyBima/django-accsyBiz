How It Works

AccsyBiz uses business license data from the Chicago Data Portal to provide information about businesses in Chicago. By analyzing accessibility attributes, we help users find businesses that cater to their needs. Simply browse the map or use the search functionality to explore accessible businesses near you. In order for an accessibility feature to be accepted, at least 2 upvotes is required.
